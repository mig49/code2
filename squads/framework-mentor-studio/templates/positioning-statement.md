## 3. DIAGNOSTIC CONVERSATION SCRIPT

**Purpose:** Demonstrate expertise through questions, not pitching.

### Opening (2 min):
"Thanks for taking the time. I'd love to understand your situation better before we discuss anything. Can I ask you a few questions?"

### Deep Questions (15 min):
1. "What's the #1 challenge you're facing with [topic] right now?"
2. "How long has this been going on?"
3. "What have you tried so far?"
4. "What do you think is the root cause?"
5. "If we solved this, what would change for you?"

### Insight (5 min):
"Based on what you've shared, I see a pattern I've seen many times. In my [Framework Name], this is typically a [Step X] problem. Here's what usually helps..."
*[Give ONE valuable insight for free]*

### Invitation (3 min):
"I have a program where I help people like you work through this systematically using my [Framework Name]. Would you be interested in exploring what that looks like?"
